Help & Support

How to use Chat?

Click on 'Companion Chat'. Wait for the widget to load. Type or speak to the AI assistant.

Setting Medicine Reminders

Go to 'Medicines'. Enter name, dosage, and time. Click Save. You will be alerted at the time.

Using SOS

Tap the big red SOS button in the SOS page. It will sound an alarm and 'notify' emergency contacts.

Changing Language

Go to Settings. Select Tamil or English from the dropdown. Save Settings.

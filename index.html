About Elder Care Companion

What is this?

Elder Care Companion is an AI-powered safety and wellness platform designed specifically for senior citizens and their support networks.

How it works?

The system uses vision-based AI for fall detection and object recognition, combined with a Large Language Model (Gemini) to provide natural, reassuring conversation and proactive health alerts.

How to use it?

1. Elder: Log in to start the companion. Keep the dashboard open for automatic safety monitoring.
2. Caregiver: Log in to receive alerts if a fall is detected or an SOS is triggered.
3. Hospital: Log in to review health trends and clinical adherence data.
